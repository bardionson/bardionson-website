# Automatic War

**Automatic War** is an early conceptual digital arts project. Comprising a tight series of three or four key pieces, the project focuses on the thematic collision between algorithmic automation and human conflict. 

As part of my ongoing exploration into how machine systems distort our realities and mediate human experiences, "Automatic War" represents a distinct visual study of systemized aggression. The pieces within this project take abstract concepts of technology-driven conflict and visualize them through the artifacts and aesthetics of computational generation. 

While a relatively small project, it marks an important thematic stepping stone in my broader portfolio of questioning and visually breaking down the digital structures that surround us.

_Original web presentations of this work were captured and archived on [Rhizome Conifer](https://conifer.rhizome.org/bard_ionson/automatic-war)._
