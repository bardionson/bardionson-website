## [Abstract Art in the Age of New Media](https://www.mocda.org/abstract-art-new-media)

##### [MOCDA Museum of Contemporary Digital Art](https://www.mocda.org)

[Press Release](https://www.mocda.org/pressrelease-abstract-art-new-media)

February- May 2021

![](https://fmxqa9jjugng70wj.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/exhibitions/image-13.png)

[Life Is Green](https://superrare.com/artwork-v2/life-is-green-19952), [Alien Life Of Venus](https://superrare.co/artwork-v2/alien-life-of-venus-13665)

A multidisciplinary team headed by two UCL researchers has been awarded a research grant from the British Academy, to unravel the psychology of how people view and remember artworks in a gallery. The collaboration between cognitive psychologists, cultural and digital sector professionals takes place in the context of an online shift for art collections worldwide.
