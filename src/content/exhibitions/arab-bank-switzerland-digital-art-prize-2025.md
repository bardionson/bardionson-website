## Arab Bank Switzerland Digital Art Prize 2025

#### June 4 – June 5 2025

Lisbon [Palácio de Tancos](https://www.palaciotancos.com/)

Bone Flag from the Vanishing Of The Genuine collection appears as one of ten finalists for the [Digital Art Prize 2025](https://www.arabbank.ch/about-us/art/abs-prize/2025-edition/)

Thank you to Sebastien Montabonel, [Marlene Corbun](https://x.com/MarleneCorbun), [Mimin Guyen](https://x.com/miminguyenmimi), [Eleonora Brizi](https://x.com/eleonorabrizi), [](https://x.com/halecar2)[Alejandro Cartagena](https://x.com/halecar2) and [Rani Jabban](https://x.com/RaniJabban) for their incredible works as jury members for this third edition.

Cezar Mocan with World Upstream was awarded the prize
