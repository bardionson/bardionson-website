## ArtCrush Gallery Digital Art On The Streets

#### Various dates between October 8 – December 1, 2024

Exhibition of six art pieces of “_**Vanishing Of The Genuine**_“

Art is being seen in

Belgium

Indonesia – Until Dec 1 2024

South Africa, Romania, Netherlands

and more

“_**Vanishing Of The Genuine**_” is a new unreleased series of art pieced created from the training of Ai using analog film images of bones floating in the sky. The theme is the disappearance of what we view as reality into a simulation.
