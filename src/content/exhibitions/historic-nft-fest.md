## Historic NFT Fest

### The Past Of The Future

October 2022 – Barcelona

The [HNFTFest 2022](https://hnft.community/) Memento is an immersive virtual recreation of the retrospective curated by Eleonora Brizi, featuring significant early artworks from 2011-2019. It was created to commemorate the first HNFTFest, which took place in Barcelona, Spain in October 2022.

Multiple pieces were highlighted including the first NFT CryptoArt Short Story [SAGE Anomaly](https://sageanomaly.com)
