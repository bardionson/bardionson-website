## Vanishing Of The Genuine

A new series premiered on the streets of Belgium, Netherlands, South Africa, Indonesia, Romania and more. Sponsored by [Art Crush Gallery](https://www.artcrush.gallery/) and various outdoor advertising companies like Clear Channel the images and videos of the series were displayed on digital screens.

The series explores the evolution of analog film photography and Ai technologies and how we might be losing what is genuine.

To the right **_Death By Meme_** in the Midi train station Brussels.

Also appearing in Indonesia, South Africa, Romania and Netherlands

Created by photographing bones and animal skulls with 35mm film and training a GAN model. Death By Meme was animated using Runway image to video AI

**art crush gallery**

## Deer Skull Pair

Created by Bård by using analog film photography to photograph bones against the sky and training many GAN models. The skull on the left _**Deer Skull Shaman**_ is generated from the model. The skull on the right **_Deer Skull Token_** is a photograph found by Bård’s wife and photographed with a half frame Olympus Pen camera.

## Romania

### Pelvis

This piece was created by training a model on flim photos from the artist of bones against the sky. The Ai model generated this image and then the artist imaged it back to analog film.
