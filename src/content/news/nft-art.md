Recently I was accepted to a new NFT art platform called Mint Gold Dust. Its meaning comes from the founder [Kelly LeValley Hunt](https://twitter.com/LeValleyKelly) who at an antiques auction saw old bags being sold for excessive amounts of money. She learned that they were once used to carry gold and now the weave of the bags was full of gold dust. The founders explain it on the [first](https://mintgolddust.podbean.com/e/episode-1-the-mint-gold-dust-origin-story-featuring-kelly-levalley-hunt-eleonora-brizi-louis-parker/) Mint Gold Dust Podcast.

I am featured on the [fifth episode](https://mintgolddust.podbean.com/e/episode-5-genesis-artist-bard-ionson/) where I talk about my practice, how I became and artist, my future epic art installation project and what mint gold dust means to me.
