Arab Bank Switzerland Digital Art Prize

The event was held at [Palácio de Tancos](https://www.palaciotancos.com/) in the middle of Lisbon Portugal and digital artists, curators, gallerists and business people from all over the world attended.

On display were ten art works by emerging digital artists. Andreas Rau with Inventory Numbers #44 aurèce vettier with la traversée de la forêt

[Bård Ionson](https://www.linkedin.com/in/bard-ionson/) with Vanishing Of The Genuine : Bone Flag [Cezar Mocan](https://www.linkedin.com/in/cezarmocan/) with World Upstream [Ivona Tau (Tautkutė – Rustecka, PhD)](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ivona-tau/) with My body is a surreal machine James Bloom with Mass #64, Kerim Safa & loackme with nullMachines++#53, [Leander Herzog](https://www.linkedin.com/in/leander-herzog-98438829b/) with Extraflip, [Marcel Schwittlick](https://www.linkedin.com/in/marcelschwittlick/) with Caltech Studies, Travis LeRoy with Substratum

### Bone Flag – Vanishing Of The Genuine collection

_Bård Ionson, Vanishing of the Genuine: Bone Flag, 2024, AI video generated through_ Generative Adversarial Network _(GAN) algorithms, utilizing a dataset of the artist’s analog film photographs of animal bones and flags, 4K resolution, looped, 00:50, Edition 1/1, minted on Ethereum_ 

This work is from Bård Ionson’s series _Bones: Vanishing Of The Genuine_, which examines how reality shifts into hyperreal simulations, challenging our perception by asking: “How do we live in a world where reality seems to be vanishing?”

THE ART GALLERY

Pioneers. Far from societal trends or passing fads, we are all pioneers.

Three years ago, Arab Bank Switzerland took its engagement in the digital art space a step further by creating the first on-chain digital art prize in the world, an evidence. This prize was created to elevate, awaken, and promote curiosity. Forever and always weaving new links. Between art and technology, by supporting artists who, in their own ways, disrupt the world.

Today, as we do every year in Lisbon, we are proud of gathering all of you in the Artist’s Villa. At the heart of the Villa, as always, is an exhibition composed of the artworks of the ten nominees of the Emerging Artist Category of the ABS Digital Art Prize.

Today, we are celebrating these ten outstanding artists, building the future of art: Andreas Rau, aurèce vettier, Bard lonson, Cezar Mocan, Ivona Tau, James Bloom, Kerim Safa & loackme, Leander Herzog, Marcel Schwittlick, Travis LeRoy Southworth.

Our convictions make us. We believe that on-chain digital art is not an ephemeral phenomenon, but a revolution in fact. The emergence of a significant artistic movement for history as a whole, and art history especially. We believe in a paradigm shift, in new means of transmission, in a wider access to culture.

Please join us in congratulating these ten nominees chosen by our amazing jury members: Alejandro Cartagena, Eleonora Brizi, Marlène Corbun, Mimi Nguyen, Rani Jabban and Sebastien Montabonel.

Let’s create, together, the future of art.

ARAB BANK
