# [Freaky Faces](https://opensea.io/collection/bards-freaky-faces)

Bard’s Freaky Faces were born out of AI. Using a system called SPADE-Face trained on celebrities faces. I learned how to manipulate the model and generate ugly freaky faces. We now have characters such as Sally, Al, Max and more.

And then I went a step further and animated them with another tool called [First Order Model](https://aliaksandrsiarohin.github.io/first-order-model-website/). So now they have something to say. Only 50 were ever created.
