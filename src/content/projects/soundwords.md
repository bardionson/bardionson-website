Sound Words The beginning of a new series not sure where this is going art just happens as I play The first Sound Words on [SuperRare](https://superrare.com/0x62e63b40ae0f1618fc8e422b8cea28a69ccd5d41/illegal-human-1) are Illegal Humans

I create these words by imagining a poem & using primary words of it to create a stereo buzz that when played on an Oscilloscope create the typed words Then I disrupt the typed words with my spoken word poem read twice once for each ear.

The sound can be transmitted and if not compressed be deciphered anywhere the sound travels. An oscilloscope or even a laser can interpret the sound in to a visual.
